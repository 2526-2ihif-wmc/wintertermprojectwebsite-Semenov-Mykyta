About the Major

The CS2 Austin Major 2025 is a landmark tournament for Counter-Strike 2, bringing the Major back to the United States. The event features the best teams from Europe, CIS, Americas, and Asia.

Played on the Source 2 engine, the Austin Major highlights modern CS2 mechanics such as dynamic smokes, updated tick system, and enhanced visuals.

Venue

The Austin Major takes place in Austin, Texas, one of the fastest-growing esports cities in North America. The modern arena allows thousands of fans to experience the Major live.
