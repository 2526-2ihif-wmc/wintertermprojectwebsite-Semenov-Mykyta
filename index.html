About the Game

Counter-Strike 2 is a tactical, team-based first-person shooter (FPS) where two teams—Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists—battle it out in various objective-based game modes. The most iconic game mode is the Bomb Defusal, where the Terrorists try to plant a bomb in one of two designated bomb sites, while the Counter-Terrorists aim to prevent the bomb from being planted or defuse it if it's already been planted.


CS2 builds upon the foundation set by its predecessors with improved graphics, new weapons, and maps, enhanced physics, and updates to its Source 2 engine. The game is known for its deep strategy, precise aiming mechanics, and highly competitive gameplay. Players need to rely on teamwork, communication, and tactics rather than just raw shooting skill.
