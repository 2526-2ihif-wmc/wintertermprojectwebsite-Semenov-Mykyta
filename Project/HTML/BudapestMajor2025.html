About the Major

The CS2 Budapest Major 2025 is one of the most prestigious esports tournaments in Counter-Strike history. Organized by Valve, it brings together the best teams from all over the world to compete for glory, prestige, and a multi-million dollar prize pool.

This Major is played on Counter-Strike 2 using the Source 2 engine, featuring updated maps, improved smoke physics, and modern visuals.

Venue

The tournament takes place in Budapest, Hungary, at a modern indoor arena designed for large-scale esports events. Thousands of fans will attend live, with millions watching online.
